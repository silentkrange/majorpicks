Rules

  1. Each team is made up of five players.
  2. Players are selected from the top 100 players in the field of each tournament.
  3. Each team can select any five players from the top 100; there is no quota for high or low ranked paleyrs.
  4. Players are ranked by the Sagarin Rankings. These are more reflective of recent play than the World Golf Rankings.
  5. Each players gets a bonus value. The value is determined by their ranking. The top ten players get zero, the next ten get minus-one, the next ten get minus-two, and so on. The largest bonus for any player is minus-nine.
  6. The goal of the game is to have the lowest score at the end of the tournament.
  7. Each team's score consists of the sum of its two best scores, plus any bonuses that Team has won.
  8. There are no tiebreakers.
  9. Good luck!
